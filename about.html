About

Aolio is a side project of mine that's intended to be a place to discuss the latest social news as well as create communities centered around any number of hobbies or interests. Currently, there exist many websites that either already do this or will do a variation of that, but none accomplish what I, as a user, would specifically want from this type of service. I want to create a user-friendly, effective, and appropriately controlled environment where people can express themselves freely and keep up to date on all those things they personally find interesting. Here's how I think this can be done:

  • Moderated and user-moderated communities
  • A clean and simple to use user interface
  • Maintaining the userbase anonymous
  • The ability to easily create and cutomize your own communities
